GET TO KNOW ME

Hello! My name is Angelica, and I am so excited you're here. I am currently based in Ann Arbor as a first-year Master's student in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. During vacation, I can be located in Chicago. I use photography as an escape from the realities of the world. Through my photography, I aim to capture the beauty of the subject(s) in an artistic and profound way to share a narrative. Each subject is different, and it is important I encapsulate their uniqueness through the camera lens. My portfolio consists of a multitude of narratives including personal portraits, nature, lanscape, and special occassions. If you would like to work with me, do not hesitate to reach out. Thank you.
